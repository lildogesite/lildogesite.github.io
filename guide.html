Step 1: Buy LILDOGE

Go to:
https://exchange.pancakeswap.finance/#/swap?outputCurrency=0xc9f09ee25f90283af9b90756850638ea03ff2e04

● Select how much BNB you want to swap for LILDOGE

● Approve LILDOGE and confirm the transaction

Step 2: Acquire LILDOGE-BNB LP Tokens

LP Tokens act as proof that you are contributing assets to a liquidity pool on PancakeSwap. You can stake them on ValueDeFi to earn more LILDOGE tokens.

Go to:
https://exchange.pancakeswap.finance/#/add/ETH/0xc9f09ee25f90283af9b90756850638ea03ff2e04

● Click “Unlock Wallet”

● Once your balance shows up, choose BNB as the other input token

● Specify the amount of BNB / LILDOGE you wish to add into the liquidity pool

● Click “Approve LILDOGE” and confirm the transaction

● Click “Supply”

● If the information is correct, click “Confirm Supply”

● After clicking “Confirm Supply”, there be a pop-up notification to confirm the transaction

● Wait for the transaction to be processed and confirmed

● Once the transaction has gone through, you will receive Pancake LP tokens in your wallet verifying that you have provided liquidity to the pool

● LILDOGE – BNB LP Token Address:
    0x291BD90e42fE021e3dDD5E8684E62b9a6537e94F

Step 3: Stake your LILDOGE – BNB LP Tokens

(https://bsc.valuedefi.io/#/vfarm/0xd5c62a7c42668d80cce6a20e71e1c5b25aecf593)
